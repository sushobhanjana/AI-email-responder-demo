# Minutes of Meeting (MoM) Policy

## Meeting Detection
Any email that is a calendar invite or discusses a scheduled call is considered a "Meeting".

**Keywords:**
- "Invitation:"
- "Meeting with"
- "Sync up"
- "Weekly Call"
- "Zoom"
- "Teams link"
- "Google Meet"

## MoM Requirements
For every formal meeting (Client calls, Project syncs, Reviews), a "Minutes of Meeting" (MoM) email must be sent within **24 hours**.

**MoM Email Identifiers:**
- Subject contains: "MoM", "Minutes", "Summary", "Action Items", "Notes from call"
- Body contains: "Attendees", "Decisions", "Next Steps"

## Exceptions
- 1:1 casual chats do not strictly require MoM.
- "Lunch" or "Coffee" events do not require MoM.

## Classification Rules
1. If email is a Meeting Invite -> Tag as "Meeting", Start Tracking.
2. If email is a MoM -> Tag as "MoM", Mark Meeting as Completed.
