# Email Analysis Policies

## Urgent Emails
- Emails from 'emergency@example.com' are always urgent.
- Emails with 'urgent' in the subject are urgent.

## General Inquiry
- Emails asking for information should be categorized as 'General Inquiry'.
- Suggested reply: 'Thank you for your inquiry. We will get back to you shortly.'

## Spam
- Emails with 'spam' in the subject should be marked as spam.
- Delete all spam emails.
