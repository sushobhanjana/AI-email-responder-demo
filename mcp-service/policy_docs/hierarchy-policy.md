# Hierarchy & Client Policy

## Internal Hierarchy
Emails from the following domains and roles are considered "Internal Hierarchy" and should be treated with appropriate priority.

**Domains:**
- @yourcompany.com
- @parentcompany.com

**Key Roles (High Priority):**
- CEO, CTO, CFO, COO
- VP of Engineering, VP of Product
- Director
- Head of Department

## Client List
Emails from these domains are considered "External Clients" and are critical for business.

**Client Domains:**
- @client-a.com
- @partner-b.org
- @enterprise-c.net

**VIP Contacts:**
- john.doe@client-a.com (Champion)
- jane.smith@partner-b.org (Decision Maker)

## Classification Rules
1. If sender is from Internal Hierarchy -> Tag as "Internal", Priority "Medium" (or "High" if Urgent).
2. If sender is from Client Domain -> Tag as "Client", Priority "High".
